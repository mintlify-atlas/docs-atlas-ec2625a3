Cheat Sheets is available for both Android and iOS platforms. Follow the installation instructions for your device below.

## Android Installation

There are two ways to install Cheat Sheets on Android devices:

### Google Play Store (Recommended)

The easiest way to install Cheat Sheets on Android is through the Google Play Store:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Google Play Store">
    Launch the Google Play Store app on your Android device
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for Cheat Sheets">
    Search for "Cheat Sheets" by ivansaul or visit the direct link:
    
    [Download on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ivansaul.cheatsheets)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install the App">
    Tap the Install button and wait for the download to complete
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Once installed, open the app from your home screen or app drawer
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Manual Installation (APK)

If you prefer to install the app manually or don't have access to Google Play:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Unknown Sources">
    Go to **Settings** → **Security** → Enable **Install unknown apps** for your browser or file manager
    
    <Warning>
      Only download APK files from trusted sources like the official GitHub repository
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download APK">
    Visit the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/ivansaul/cheat-sheets/releases/latest) and download the latest `.apk` file
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install">
    Open the downloaded APK file and follow the installation prompts
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Once installed, open the app from your home screen
  </Step>
</Steps>

## iOS Installation

On iOS, Cheat Sheets is distributed via **AltStore**, which allows you to sideload apps without jailbreaking your device.

<Note>
  AltStore requires a Mac or Windows PC for the initial setup. The app will need to be refreshed every 7 days (free Apple ID) or 365 days (paid Apple Developer account).
</Note>

### Prerequisites

- A Mac or Windows PC
- An iPhone or iPad running iOS 12.0 or later
- An Apple ID
- A USB cable to connect your device to your computer

### Step 1: Install AltStore

First, you need to install AltStore on your computer and iOS device:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="macOS">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download AltServer">
        Download AltServer for macOS from the [official AltStore website](https://faq.altstore.io/altstore-classic/how-to-install-altstore-macos)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install AltServer">
        Open the downloaded file and drag AltServer to your Applications folder
      </Step>
      <Step title="Run AltServer">
        Launch AltServer from your Applications folder. It will appear in your menu bar
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install AltStore on iOS">
        Connect your iOS device via USB, click the AltServer icon in the menu bar, select **Install AltStore**, then choose your device
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Apple ID">
        Enter your Apple ID credentials when prompted (these are only used locally to sign the app)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Trust Developer">
        On your iOS device, go to **Settings** → **General** → **VPN & Device Management** → Trust your Apple ID
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    For detailed instructions, watch this [video guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=AtFcvxCBt-A)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Windows">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Install iTunes and iCloud">
        Download and install the latest versions of iTunes and iCloud from Apple's website
        
        <Warning>
          Use the versions from Apple, not the Microsoft Store versions
        </Warning>
      </Step>
      <Step title="Download AltServer">
        Download AltServer for Windows from the [official AltStore website](https://faq.altstore.io/altstore-classic/how-to-install-altstore-windows)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install AltServer">
        Run the installer and complete the setup
      </Step>
      <Step title="Run AltServer">
        Launch AltServer from the Start menu. It will appear in your system tray
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install AltStore on iOS">
        Connect your iOS device via USB, right-click the AltServer icon in the system tray, select **Install AltStore**, then choose your device
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Apple ID">
        Enter your Apple ID credentials when prompted
      </Step>
      <Step title="Trust Developer">
        On your iOS device, go to **Settings** → **General** → **VPN & Device Management** → Trust your Apple ID
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    For detailed instructions, watch this [video guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MY_2WqlzrHE)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Step 2: Install Cheat Sheets

Once AltStore is installed on your iOS device, you can install Cheat Sheets:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="AltStore Source (Recommended)">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open AltStore">
        Launch the AltStore app on your iOS device
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add Source">
        Tap **Browse** → **Sources** → **+** (top right)
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Source URL">
        Enter this URL: `https://tinyurl.com/ivansaulAltstore`
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install Cheat Sheets">
        Find Cheat Sheets in the source and tap **Install**
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Manual IPA Installation">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download IPA">
        On your iOS device, visit the [GitHub Releases page](https://github.com/ivansaul/cheat-sheets/releases/latest) and download the `.ipa` file
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open in AltStore">
        Tap the downloaded file and select **Open in AltStore**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install">
        AltStore will sign and install the app on your device
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
  **Important:** After installation, do NOT open the app using the "OPEN" button in AltStore. This may cause the app to not work correctly due to a deeplink issue. Instead, open the app from your home screen icon. This will be fixed in a future update.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

### Android Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="App won't install from APK">
    - Ensure you've enabled "Install unknown apps" for your browser or file manager
    - Check that you have enough storage space
    - Verify you downloaded the correct APK for your device architecture
    - Try clearing your download cache and re-downloading the APK
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="App crashes on startup">
    - Clear the app cache: **Settings** → **Apps** → **Cheat Sheets** → **Storage** → **Clear Cache**
    - Ensure you're running Android 5.0 or higher
    - Try uninstalling and reinstalling the app
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### iOS Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="AltStore installation failed">
    - Ensure iTunes and iCloud are installed (Windows) or that you have the latest macOS
    - Make sure your device is connected via USB and unlocked
    - Verify WiFi is enabled on both your computer and iOS device
    - Try restarting AltServer and your iOS device
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="App won't open after installation">
    - Make sure you're opening the app from the home screen, NOT from the "OPEN" button in AltStore
    - Go to **Settings** → **General** → **VPN & Device Management** and verify your Apple ID is trusted
    - Try refreshing the app in AltStore
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="App expired / won't open">
    - Free Apple IDs require apps to be refreshed every 7 days
    - Connect to the same WiFi as your computer running AltServer
    - Open AltStore and tap **Refresh All** on the My Apps page
    - Consider using a paid Apple Developer account for 365-day certificates
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
